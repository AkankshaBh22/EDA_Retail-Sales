# EDA_Retail-Sales
Retail Sales Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using Python to uncover customer purchasing behavior, seasonal sales trends, product performance, and actionable business insights through data visualization and statistical analysis.

Objectives
Analyze customer purchasing behavior across different age groups and genders.
Identify seasonal trends in sales and transaction volume.
Explore product category performance and customer preferences.
Study the relationship between customer age, spending, and purchasing habits.
Examine transaction patterns based on purchase quantity.
Analyze product price distributions across different categories.
Generate actionable business insights and recommendations.

Key Insights
Identified peak sales periods and seasonal purchasing trends.
Analyzed customer preferences across different product categories.
Explored spending behavior across various age groups.
Evaluated transaction patterns and purchase quantities.
Generated data-driven recommendations for inventory management, marketing, and customer segmentation.

Learning Outcomes
Performed a complete real-world EDA project.
Applied feature engineering techniques to create meaningful variables.
Developed professional data visualizations using Matplotlib and Seaborn.
Converted raw data into actionable business insights.
Strengthened analytical storytelling and business interpretation skills.
